Testimonial

What Our Clients Says About Us

We are a small group of debt counsellors representing both small and large debt counselling firms that have decided to take on what we believe is an unfair framework enforced by the National Credit Regulator (NCR) towards registered and abiding debt counsellors. They have made it in many cases impossible for debt counsellors to run profitable practises and have over the years imposed and enforced varies fines and penalties on debt counsellors which we believe is not in the best interest of the debt counsellor or the industry. If no one fights for change and a fair playing field, then the industry will not be sustainable. Collectively we can change this, we strongly believe that if we do not act now, the future of debt counsellors will look very bleak soon. We have established a strong panel of attorneys and advocates that will represent our case to bring change in the industry and fight specifically for the rights of debt counsellors. No other body or institution have done what we have decided to embark on, and that is to fight for the rights of debt counsellors and to bring change to the industry, none of which will be to the determine of the consumer.

As you can imagine, we are taking on a goliath, as the NCR holds the mandate to regulate the industry and have the budget of government to back them. This have led to an environment where debt counsellors who do not have the financial backing have gone under or left the industry where the NCR have made it impossible for them to operate in and even imposed fines on such debt counsellors. The NCR has even gone as far as confiscation debt counsellor’s client books without compensation and have distributed it to other firms of their choice. We believe this needs to change and justice needs to be served for debt counsellors. This can only be done with your input and participation in our envisioned class-action lawsuit against the NCR. Only through this will we be able to bring change to the industry. There is no legal cost involved to participating debt counsellors, all that we ask is that you take the time to complete our Petition For Declaratory Relief Form. That is all it will cost you, your time, and hopefully through your input and participation we can bring change and justice in the industry and secure a future for the business that you have worked for so hard for. We invite existing and previous debt counsellors to join our petition and bring much needed change!
